## Key Tactics
- Compare current month performance against the previous month to identify major fluctuations and trends in your Google Ads account.
- Conduct year-over-year analysis to spot significant performance drops; a 20% decline warrants investigation even when costs remain stable.
- When revenue declines year-over-year, drill down by campaign type (brand, search, Performance Max) to identify where the performance loss is concentrated.
- Look for "leakage" across multiple campaigns rather than assuming a single problem; this helps determine if the issue is account-wide or isolated.
- Distinguish between macro-level market issues and account-specific problems (e.g., Performance Max campaign deterioration) to focus your troubleshooting efforts.

## Summary
Analyzing Google Ads performance month-over-month and year-over-year helps identify significant trends and revenue drops that require investigation. By comparing periods and drilling down into individual campaign performance, you can pinpoint whether declines are widespread or isolated to specific campaign types or channels. This systematic performance review is essential for maintaining account health and catching issues before they impact revenue significantly.
