## Key Tactics
- Use demographic exclusions in Performance Max to exclude underperforming age groups (e.g., 18-24) at campaign launch when you have clear knowledge of your target audience.
- Exclude tablet traffic from new Performance Max campaigns initially, then optimize performance on mobile and desktop before gradually opening tablet traffic back up.
- Leverage device-level exclusions as a diagnostic tool for underperforming campaigns—removing low-performing devices allows you to isolate and improve core traffic sources before expanding.
- Apply these new exclusion controls at campaign launch rather than waiting for performance data, especially when you have prior knowledge of which demographics or devices your business should avoid.

## Summary
Google has added demographic and device exclusion controls to Performance Max campaigns, giving advertisers more granular audience targeting capabilities previously available only in Shopping and Search. These new controls allow advertisers to exclude underperforming age groups and devices (particularly tablets) at launch, improving campaign performance by filtering traffic to higher-converting segments before scaling.
