## Key Tactics
- Create promotions in Google Merchant Center under the Marketing > Promotions section to automatically add green sale badges and icons to your shopping product ads, which increases click-through rates.
- Schedule promotions for specific date ranges in Merchant Center so sale pricing and icons display only during active promotional periods (e.g., Black Friday).
- Ensure promotions are set up in Merchant Center rather than just in campaigns, as this is where product ads pull sale badge and pricing update signals from.
- Use sale badges strategically during high-intent shopping periods like Black Friday when users are actively searching for deals and discounts.

## Summary
Adding Black Friday and promotional sale badges to Google Shopping ads through Merchant Center promotions increases visibility and click-through rates by displaying eye-catching green sale icons to deal-hunting customers. Set up promotions in the Marketing section of Merchant Center and schedule them for your active promotional dates to ensure sale badges appear on your product ads at the right time.
