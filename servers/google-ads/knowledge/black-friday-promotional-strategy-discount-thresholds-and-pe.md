## Key Tactics
- Avoid running promotions below 20% discount during Black Friday and Cyber Monday; smaller discounts (5%) don't justify the promotional effort and won't meaningfully increase sales beyond baseline holiday shopping behavior.
- Sitewide sales are the most effective promotional approach based on analysis of 700+ stores; alternative tactics like free shipping, buy-X-get-Y deals, or select SKU discounts are less proven but viable options.
- Use Performance Max campaigns for Black Friday promotions—the speaker's agency has generated over $50 million in revenue using this campaign type during peak shopping periods.
- When planning holiday promotions, benchmark against industry data and best practices from successful retailers rather than setting arbitrary discount levels based on internal margins alone.

## Summary
Black Friday promotions should only be executed with discounts of at least 20% to justify the campaign investment, with sitewide sales being the most effective approach across tested retailers. Performance Max campaigns are the recommended bidding strategy for maximizing revenue during this high-intent shopping period.
