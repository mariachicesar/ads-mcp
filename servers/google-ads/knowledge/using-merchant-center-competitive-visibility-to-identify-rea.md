## Key Tactics
- Access the Competitive Visibility report in Merchant Center under Performance tab to identify your actual shopping competitors without leaving the platform.
- Filter competitors by similar visibility levels rather than absolute visibility; this helps you focus on realistic competitors like yourself rather than dominant players like Amazon or Walmart.
- Use Merchant Center's Competitive Visibility data (page overlap rate, position ratio, organic ratio) as a supplement to Google Ads auction insights for a more complete competitive picture.
- Customize the timeframe and country filters in the Competitive Visibility report to match your campaign geography and analysis period for accurate competitor benchmarking.

## Summary
The Merchant Center Competitive Visibility report provides a quick, one-minute way to identify your real shopping competitors by showing relative auction performance, page overlap rates, and position data—complementing the auction insights available within Google Ads. By focusing on competitors with similar visibility levels rather than dominant players, you get actionable competitive intelligence specific to your market segment.
