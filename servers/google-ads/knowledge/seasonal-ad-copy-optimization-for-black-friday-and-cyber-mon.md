## Key Tactics
- Add Black Friday and Cyber Monday-specific ad copy to campaigns you expect to perform strongly during these peak shopping periods to instantly boost click-through rates.
- Include seasonal messaging like "Black Friday Sale" or "Cyber Monday Deal" directly in ad copy rather than generic promotional language to increase relevance and urgency.
- Consider alternative seasonal angles (e.g., "Better than Black Friday" or "Fall Clearance Sale") if direct seasonal terms feel overused, but maintain the seasonal context in messaging.
- Test seasonal ad copy variations in high-traffic ad groups during Q4 to capture incremental CTR improvements during peak conversion windows.

## Summary
Incorporating Black Friday and Cyber Monday terminology directly into ad copy for relevant campaigns during these shopping periods significantly increases click-through rates. Simple seasonal messaging tweaks can drive measurable performance improvements without requiring campaign restructuring.
