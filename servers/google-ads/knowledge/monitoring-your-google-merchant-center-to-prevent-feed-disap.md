## Key Tactics
- Check your Merchant Center account weekly for product disapprovals and feed issues, as these problems can go unnoticed in Performance Max campaigns since the algorithm will shift spend to other placements (search, display) rather than showing a dramatic performance drop.
- Set up a regular audit process to review your product feed status in Merchant Center; with thousands of products, disapprovals can accumulate quickly and silently tank your shopping campaign ROI without immediate visibility.
- Understand that Performance Max's flexibility masks feed problems—it will reallocate budget away from underperforming shopping placements—whereas Standard Shopping campaigns show immediate drops in clicks/impressions when products are disapproved, making issues easier to spot.
- Establish a workflow to investigate and resolve disapproved products as soon as they appear in your Merchant Center dashboard, rather than waiting for revenue impact to signal a problem.

## Summary
Regularly monitoring your Google Merchant Center feed for disapprovals is critical because Performance Max campaigns will silently shift spending to other channels when products are disapproved, masking the problem until significant revenue loss occurs. Standard Shopping campaigns expose feed issues faster through visible drops in performance, making proactive weekly Merchant Center checks essential for protecting campaign health.
