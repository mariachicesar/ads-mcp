## Key Tactics
- Use Merchant Center feed rules to prepend and append keywords to product titles without modifying your source data, enabling large-scale optimization across hundreds of SKUs simultaneously.
- Add high-intent qualifiers like "kid and pet friendly" and "washable" to the front of titles to match customer search behavior and improve relevance for specific audience segments.
- Include brand names at the end of titles to build trust and improve CTR, transforming generic titles like "tucks standard sectional in multi soda" into keyword-rich, benefit-focused titles.
- Test title performance by using the "show preview" feature in Merchant Center to see how feed rules impact the final title display before applying changes to your entire product feed.

## Summary
Google Shopping campaign performance depends heavily on product title optimization. By leveraging Merchant Center feed rules to dynamically prepend customer-focused attributes and append brand names, you can dramatically improve ad relevance and conversion rates across your entire product catalog without editing individual product feeds.
