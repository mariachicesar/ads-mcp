## Key Tactics
- Use the Performance Max campaign modification request form to add negative keywords beyond Google's 100-keyword limit per campaign, since the built-in limitation is unnecessarily restrictive compared to other campaign types.
- Exercise caution with the high-value customer acquisition mode feature, as it may increase CPCs without delivering proportional value and doesn't reliably distinguish between new and returning customers.
- Avoid relying on brand exclusions from product feed campaigns if you need precise control—manually manage brand terms separately instead, keeping them active in shopping ads while excluding them from search when needed.

## Summary
Google has released three new Performance Max features: campaign-level negative keywords (capped at 100), high-value customer acquisition bidding, and brand exclusions from product feeds. While negative keywords are a useful addition, the artificial limits and concerns about the other features' effectiveness suggest careful evaluation before implementation.
