## Key Tactics
- Access the Price Competitiveness report in Google Merchant Center's Growth section to benchmark your product prices against competitor averages identified in your shopping ads data.
- Identify products where you're significantly above benchmark pricing (e.g., 13% higher) as immediate optimization targets, since these items are unlikely to win clicks against cheaper competitors in shopping results.
- Cross-reference products with high click volume to confirm you're generally competitive on price; conversely, products with declining clicks and red flags in the report signal pricing competitiveness issues.
- Use price competitiveness gaps as conversation starters with clients or internal teams to investigate root causes—whether pricing strategy, cost structure, or margin targets need adjustment.
- Treat product pricing data as actionable intelligence for your shopping ads strategy, not just a reporting metric; use insights to guide both bidding decisions and business-level pricing discussions.

## Summary
Google Merchant Center's Price Competitiveness report reveals how your product prices compare to competitor benchmarks, helping you identify which items are priced too high to compete effectively in shopping ads. By analyzing products with declining clicks and red flags indicating above-benchmark pricing, you can pinpoint optimization opportunities and make data-driven pricing decisions that improve click-through rates and revenue.
