## Key Tactics
- Replace GA4 as your primary conversion action with a native Google Ads purchase tag or Shopify Google YouTube app integration; GA4 attributes 10-15% less conversion value to Google Ads compared to native tracking methods.
- Link your GA4 account to Google Ads, but set it to a secondary conversion action for reporting and analysis purposes while using the native tag as your primary optimization target.
- For Shopify stores, prioritize the Shopify Google YouTube app integration over GA4 imports to capture more accurate attribution data that Google Ads can optimize against.
- The discrepancy between GA4 and native Google Ads attribution stems from different tracking methodologies; native tags directly capture user interactions within the Google Ads ecosystem, resulting in more accurate revenue attribution for campaign optimization.

## Summary
Using GA4 as your primary conversion action in Google Ads results in leaving 10-15% of attributable revenue on the table. Instead, implement native Google Ads purchase tags (or Shopify integrations) as your primary conversion action while keeping GA4 linked as a secondary source for additional insights. This approach ensures your Google Ads campaigns optimize against the most accurate conversion data available.
