## Key Tactics
- Enable AI Max for Search in campaign settings to access the search terms and landing pages report showing which queries and landing pages are being triggered by the AI Max feature.
- Monitor the search terms generated by AI Max closely in the first weeks after activation, as the system may show irrelevant or mismatched queries that don't align with your actual product offerings.
- Cross-reference AI Max-generated search terms against your existing broad match keyword performance to identify whether AI Max is delivering incremental traffic or redundant coverage you're already capturing.
- Consider disabling AI Max if it consistently generates poor-quality matches or search terms unrelated to your core offerings, even if some positive volume is present.
- If using broad match with smart bidding simultaneously, evaluate whether AI Max adds meaningful new traffic or primarily duplicates what your current strategy already achieves.

## Summary
AI Max for Search can be enabled within search campaign settings to surface new search queries and landing pages, but real-account performance shows mixed results with significant mismatches and irrelevant queries appearing. Before fully committing to AI Max, audit its generated search terms against your existing broad match and smart bidding performance to determine if it delivers incremental value or should be disabled to avoid wasted spend.
