## Key Tactics
- Review geo targeting and ad scheduling settings on a quarterly basis, even if they appear optimal, to catch unintended changes or exclusions that may have been forgotten.
- Use state-level geo targeting for more granular control, which works effectively alongside smart bidding strategies.
- Document reasons for any geo exclusions or schedule modifications so you can identify when settings need to be reverted to their original configuration.
- Establish a quarterly audit cadence rather than constant monitoring—this prevents setting drift without requiring excessive manual oversight.

## Summary
Reviewing your Google Ads geo targeting and ad schedule settings quarterly is essential because teams often forget to revert temporary changes, such as excluded states or modified schedules. While daily monitoring isn't necessary, a quarterly check ensures your campaigns maintain their intended configuration and continue to perform optimally.
