## Key Tactics
- Navigate to Merchant Center settings > General Account Options and disable "Marketing Content Usage" by selecting "Do not share data" to prevent Google from automatically pulling promotions from your email marketing campaigns into your shopping ads.
- Recognize that email marketing and Google Ads serve different strategic purposes for most clients; allowing Google to automatically sync email promotions to shopping ads can create confusion and dilute the effectiveness of both channels.
- Opt out of Google's automated promotion features by default, as past experience shows these features often create more confusion than incremental value for accounts.

## Summary
Google now automatically syncs promotional content from your marketing emails to your shopping ads unless you opt out. The recommendation is to disable this feature in Merchant Center settings because email marketing and paid search serve different strategic roles, and automatic promotion syncing typically creates confusion rather than value.
