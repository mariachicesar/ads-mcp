## Key Tactics
- Use Merchant Center's attribute rules feature to automatically segment seasonal products by creating custom labels based on product title keywords (e.g., "Christmas," "Santa," "holiday").
- Navigate to Data Sources > Content API > Attribute Rules to set up automated rules that identify seasonal products without manual feed management.
- Set up conditional rules that scan product titles for seasonal keywords and assign them to custom label fields (like custom_label_3), enabling easy campaign segmentation.
- Use the "Show Preview" function to validate your attribute rules before applying them to ensure the conditions correctly identify your seasonal inventory.
- Properly segmented seasonal products allow you to create targeted Shopping campaigns that scale faster and respond to seasonal demand without constant manual feed updates.

## Summary
This video demonstrates how to automate seasonal product segmentation in Google Merchant Center using attribute rules, allowing you to quickly create custom labels for Christmas, holiday, and other seasonal products. By setting conditional rules based on product title keywords, you can organize your feed dynamically and enable faster scaling of Shopping campaigns without manual intervention.
