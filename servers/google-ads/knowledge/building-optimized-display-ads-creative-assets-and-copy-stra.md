## Key Tactics
- Upload the maximum 15 images to display ads whenever possible, ensuring all images are properly optimized for quality and relevance to maximize creative variety and performance.
- Leverage suggested headlines that Google pulls directly from your landing page to save time and ensure message consistency between ad copy and destination.
- Maximize all five available headline slots in display ads, using up to 30 characters per regular headline and 90 characters for the long headline to capture user attention.
- Utilize all five description fields (90 characters each) to communicate key value propositions and drive specific calls-to-action that align with your landing page offer.
- Include a clear call-to-action in your ad descriptions (e.g., "Comment PMax below" or similar) to encourage user engagement and make the next step obvious.

## Summary
When creating display ads, maximize creative assets by uploading all available images and filling every headline and description slot with optimized, landing-page-aligned copy. This approach increases the number of ad variations Google can test, improving reach and performance across different placements and formats.
