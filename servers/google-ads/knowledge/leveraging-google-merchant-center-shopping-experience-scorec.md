## Key Tactics
- Access the Shopping Experience Scorecard in Merchant Center via the Overview tab or Growth section to receive Google's quality rating and identify optimization opportunities.
- Monitor your store's badge status (e.g., "Top Quality Store") as it directly signals competitiveness and quality to Google and impacts ad eligibility and performance.
- Analyze scorecard metrics across shipping experience, return experience, seller rating, and purchasing experience to identify gaps compared to category averages.
- Prioritize fixing low-scoring areas like return shipping costs, as Google uses these factors to rank stores and determine badge eligibility.
- Maintain top-quality store status to ensure your Performance Max campaigns have access to premium inventory and placement opportunities, which correlates with higher revenue generation.

## Summary
Google's Shopping Experience Scorecard in Merchant Center provides crucial feedback on your store's quality across shipping, returns, ratings, and purchasing experience. Achieving and maintaining a "Top Quality Store" badge demonstrates competitiveness to Google and directly impacts your shopping ad performance and eligibility for features like Performance Max campaigns.
