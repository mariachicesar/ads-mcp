## Key Tactics
- Reduce Performance Max spend significantly (up to 80%) when it's underperforming relative to Shopping campaigns, and reallocate that budget to higher-performing channels like Shopping.
- Monitor channel-specific spend trends year-over-year to identify which campaigns are driving conversion value most efficiently and adjust budget allocation accordingly.
- Even with identical promotional offers and deals compared to the previous year, strategic budget reallocation between campaign types can increase overall conversion value by 45%+ while reducing total spend.
- Use conversion value as the primary metric when evaluating channel performance, not just spend increases—focus on maximizing return on ad spend across the account rather than maintaining spend levels.

## Summary
By shifting budget away from underperforming Performance Max campaigns and increasing investment in Shopping campaigns, this toy and clothing retailer achieved a 45% increase in conversion value during November while actually reducing overall Performance Max spend by 80%. The strategy demonstrates that optimizing campaign-type allocation—even without changing product offerings or promotions—can significantly improve account-wide ROAS.
