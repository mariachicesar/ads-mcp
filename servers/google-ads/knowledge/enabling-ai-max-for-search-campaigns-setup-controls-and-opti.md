## Key Tactics
- Enable AI Max for Search by toggling the setting in campaign settings; this automatically activates Final URL expansion and text customization features to optimize your campaign with AI-driven technology.
- Text customization allows Google to automatically generate headlines and descriptions for your ads; you can disable this feature if you prefer to maintain full control over ad copy.
- Final URL expansion sends traffic to pages Google predicts will perform better; disable this if your existing landing pages convert well or if you want to avoid the task of excluding underperforming URLs.
- When you disable both text customization and Final URL expansion, AI Max essentially functions as broad match keyword technology, giving you more control over campaign mechanics.
- Test Final URL expansion cautiously—monitor which pages Google sends traffic to and be prepared to exclude poor-performing URLs if the feature sends visitors to suboptimal landing pages.

## Summary
AI Max for Search is a new campaign setting that automatically optimizes your search campaigns using AI, with optional features for text customization and Final URL expansion. You can selectively enable or disable these features based on your campaign needs—for example, disabling Final URL expansion if your existing pages already convert well. The feature essentially combines AI optimization with broad match keyword technology for flexible, performance-focused campaign management.
