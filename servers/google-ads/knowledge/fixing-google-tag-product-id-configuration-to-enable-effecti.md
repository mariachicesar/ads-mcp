## Key Tactics
- Check your Google tag configuration in Tools > Data Manager > View Stats to verify the e-commerce product ID parameter is being captured correctly.
- Ensure the product ID parameter is properly implemented in your Google tag; without it, dynamic remarketing ads will show random products instead of the specific products users viewed.
- Review the last 30 days of Google tag data to confirm product ID hits are being recorded; zero hits indicates a configuration issue that's preventing dynamic ads from functioning effectively.
- Implement product ID parameters correctly so dynamic product ads can show users the exact products they previously viewed on your website, improving relevance and conversion rates.

## Summary
Most Google Ads accounts have their Google tag product ID parameter misconfigured, which breaks dynamic remarketing campaigns by showing random products instead of the ones users actually viewed. Checking your Google tag settings in the Data Manager and ensuring product ID parameters are properly captured is essential for dynamic remarketing ads to function correctly and drive revenue.
