## Key Tactics
- Review your Google Merchant Center daily leading up to Black Friday to identify and resolve product issues before peak selling season.
- Use the "Needs Attention" section in Merchant Center Next to quickly surface product feed errors such as missing prices, price mismatches, and accessibility issues.
- Prioritize fixing unapproved products and data quality problems in your product feeds, as these directly impact your Shopping and Performance Max campaign eligibility and performance.
- Conduct a pre-holiday audit of your entire product inventory to ensure all items have accurate pricing, complete required fields, and proper formatting before major shopping events.

## Summary
Regularly monitor your Google Merchant Center for product feed errors and unapproved items, especially before high-traffic shopping events like Black Friday. Use the "Needs Attention" dashboard to quickly identify and fix issues like missing prices, price mismatches, and accessibility problems that could harm your Shopping and Performance Max campaign performance.
