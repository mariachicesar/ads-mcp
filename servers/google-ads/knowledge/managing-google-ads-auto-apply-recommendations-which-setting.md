## Key Tactics
- Access Auto Apply Recommendations through the Recommendations tab at the account level, not through the Optimization Score—this is the only place to control automatic changes Google can make to your account.
- Keep most Auto Apply Recommendations unchecked (disabled) to maintain control over your account; only enable a select few recommendation types to prevent Google from making unwanted automatic changes.
- Review the 23 total recommendation types available (9 under "maintain your ads" and 14 under "grow your business")—each checked box grants Google automatic control, so be intentional about which ones you enable.
- Use a checklist or cheat sheet to determine which specific Auto Apply Recommendations are safe to enable for your account strategy, rather than leaving them on by default.

## Summary
Google's Auto Apply Recommendations can automatically modify your account if enabled, which can harm performance if not carefully managed. Most Auto Apply Recommendations should remain disabled, with only a few strategically selected recommendations turned on based on a vetted checklist. Proper control over these settings is essential to protect your account from unwanted automated changes by Google.
