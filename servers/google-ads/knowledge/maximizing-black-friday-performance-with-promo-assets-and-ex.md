## Key Tactics
- Add promotional assets (like "25% site-wide" or "Buy One Get One Free") to your ads and schedule them to run specifically on Black Friday and Cyber Monday to capture high-intent traffic.
- Create Black Friday-specific sitelink extensions rather than using generic ones—customize anchor text and descriptions to highlight seasonal deals (e.g., "Black Friday Deals" instead of standard site links).
- Implement price assets to showcase product ranges and discounts (e.g., "LG TVs from $550, Samsung TVs from $450") to give customers immediate visibility into your offerings and competitive positioning.
- Ensure all promo assets are properly scheduled and active only during your promotional windows to avoid wasting budget on non-promotional periods.

## Summary
To maximize performance during Black Friday and Cyber Monday, leverage promotional assets, create seasonal-specific sitelink extensions, and add price assets to give customers immediate visibility into deals. These tactics increase ad relevance and click-through rates by making promotional messaging more prominent and specific to the selling period.
