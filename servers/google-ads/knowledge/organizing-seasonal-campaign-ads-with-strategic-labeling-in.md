## Key Tactics
- Create descriptive labels for seasonal campaigns (e.g., "Black Friday 2025") to organize ads by launch date and prevent premature or off-season activation.
- Use the label creation feature in the ads section by clicking the label button and entering a standardized naming convention that includes the campaign type and year for easy identification.
- Add labels to relevant ads by selecting the label checkbox after creation to ensure proper categorization across your account.
- Enable the "Label" column in your account metrics view by clicking "Modify Columns" so you can easily see which ads are tagged with seasonal labels at a glance.
- Implement consistent labeling conventions across your account to streamline management of time-sensitive campaigns and prevent budget waste on off-season promotions.

## Summary
Strategic labeling of seasonal ads like Black Friday and Cyber Monday campaigns helps organize your account and prevents ads from running outside their intended promotional windows. By creating labeled categories and displaying them in your metrics columns, you can quickly identify and manage time-sensitive campaigns at scale.
