## Key Tactics
- Review the Merchant Center Performance > Promotions report to identify which promotions generated the most clicks and actual sales impact, rather than assuming all promotions perform equally.
- Analyze promotion metrics including impressions, clicks, and click-through rate to determine which promotion types resonate with your audience and drive measurable revenue.
- Use historical promotion performance data to inform future campaign strategy—replicate the structure and messaging of high-performing promotions to achieve similar results.
- Distinguish between promotions that drive volume (clicks) versus those that actually move sales and revenue, since high click counts don't always correlate with profitability.

## Summary
Google Merchant Center's promotion performance report reveals which shopping promotions actually drive revenue, helping merchants move beyond guesswork to data-driven promotion strategy. By analyzing clicks, impressions, and click-through rates on past promotions, you can identify winning promotion types and replicate them for future campaigns. This insight allows merchants to optimize promotional budgets and improve overall campaign ROI.
