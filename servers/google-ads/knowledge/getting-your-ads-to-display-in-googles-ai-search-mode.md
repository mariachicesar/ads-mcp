## Key Tactics
- To display ads in Google's AI search mode, you must run broad match keywords in at least one search campaign—this is a foundational requirement for any text ad coverage in AI mode.
- Enable AI max for search in your Google Ads account; this feature is essential for unlocking AI mode eligibility and should be activated alongside your existing campaigns.
- Run either a Performance Max or Shopping campaign (or both) to qualify for ad coverage in AI mode; text ads require all three conditions (broad match + AI max + PMAX/Shopping), while shopping ads alone can show with just a Shopping campaign.
- Prioritize shopping feed hygiene above all else for e-commerce accounts—ensure your product feed is completely up to date and optimized, as a poor-quality feed will severely limit ad performance in AI mode and Shopping campaigns.
- Be aware that AI mode ad availability is currently limited to the US market, so campaigns targeting other regions will not benefit from this inventory.

## Summary
To get your ads showing in Google's AI search mode, you need to run broad match keywords, enable AI max for search, and operate either Performance Max or Shopping campaigns. For e-commerce businesses, maintaining a clean, optimized product feed is critical to capture the available AI mode inventory.
